  • Ideal for dynamic product needs or iterative R&D
  • Transparent time tracking with detailed work logs
  • Perfect for maintenance, sprints, or ad-hoc feature requests

  • Full control over planning, tech stack, tools, and workflows
  • Scale up or down based on velocity and product phase
  • Seamless alignment with your internal culture and communication
